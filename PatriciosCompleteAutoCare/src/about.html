Over 35 Years of Automotive Excellence

Patricio's Complete Auto Care has proudly served the community since 1991, providing dependable automotive services built on trust, craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction. What began as a commitment to honest repairs and quality workmanship has grown into a respected automotive service center dedicated to helping generations of drivers keep their vehicles safe and reliable.

For more than three decades, our team has focused on delivering exceptional service while treating every customer with integrity, transparency, and respect. From routine maintenance to complex repairs and collision services, we strive to provide solutions that keep our customers confidently on the road.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted automotive repair and collision service provider in our community by consistently delivering quality workmanship, innovation, and outstanding customer experiences.

Our Mission

To provide reliable automotive repair, maintenance, and collision services with honesty, professionalism, and attention to detail while building lasting relationships with every customer we serve.
